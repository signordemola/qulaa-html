NMRV Worm Gear Reducers Gearbox

Vatidel NMRV Worm gear reducers provide market advance solution with efficiency and flexibility. NMRV worm gear series also available as compact integral helical/worm option, has been designed with a view to modularity: low number of basic models can be applied to a wide range of power ratings guaranteeing top performance and reduction ratios from 5 to 1000.
