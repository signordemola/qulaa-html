About

Qulaa

The team at Qulaa is united on continuing to build upon our reputation for excellence and unsurpassed engineering and flexible approach to drive innovation.
Qulaa is the power transmission manufacturing company that helps machines to come to alive. We have been challenging speed to help embed excellence motion into the machines and bring factory to live. That means reliable and durable products from Qulaa is needed for our customers to improve performance, reduce risk and achieve sustainable growth.

Pioneer towards 50 years Qulaa started from a gear reducer factory in Taiwan to a global manufacturing excellence cooperation in cyclo drive, gear motor, precise planetary, gearbox and other power transmission parts. Throughout the years we provide customer solutions for power transmission needs with introducing innovation and durable products. Qulaa organized everything you need to drive performance for your systems: A superior family of products, unsurpassed engineering, technical support, and innovative new technologies. We invite you to experience the Qulaa difference.
